PXLPAL is a free, client-side image toolkit. Convert images between JPG, PNG, WebP and HEIC formats, compress them to reduce file size, resize to exact dimensions, crop, censor sensitive regions, rotate, flip, and adjust brightness, contrast, saturation and sharpness — all without uploading your files anywhere. Works entirely in your browser. No account, no server, no cost.
